Tesseract OCR:
Technology: Tesseract is a traditional OCR engine that uses a rule-based and
pattern matching approach: It analyzes character shapes and features to
recognize text.
Strengths:
Mature and well-established; with a long history of development:
Supports a wide range of languages (over 100)
Can be highly accurate; especially when trained with specific datasets:
Generally performs well on clean; well-structured documents with clear text.
Often performs better on CPU machines for speed.
Limitations:
Can struggle with low-quality images; varied fonts, and complex layouts
May require more pre-processing of images for optimal results:
Less effective with handwritten text or text in natural scenes
